# üìã EXTENDED DEEP-DIVE QUESTIONS (If time permits - 10-15 min additional)

### Training Process Deep-Dive
1. "Show me your current training materials. How often do you update them?"
2. "How do you handle menu changes? New seasonal items? Daily specials?"
3. "Do you test menu knowledge? How? How often? What happens if someone fails?"
4. "What's the #1 thing that makes a new hire successful vs. unsuccessful?"

### Technology & Tools
5. "What restaurant tech do you currently use?" (POS, scheduling, etc.)
6. "Have you tried any training software before? What happened?"
7. "How tech-savvy is your team? Comfortable with apps/phones for work?"

### Metrics & Performance
8. "Do you track individual server performance? What metrics matter?" (sales/cover, guest satisfaction, error rates)
9. "How do you identify your best servers? What makes them great?"
10. "What percentage of your servers would you rate as 'excellent' vs. 'adequate'?"

### Organizational Dynamics
11. "Do you promote from within? What's the typical career path?"
12. "Why do servers leave? Exit interview insights?"
13. "What would make this a 'must-have' vs. 'nice-to-have' for you?"

### Competition & Alternatives
14. "How do other restaurants in your category handle training? Do you know?"
15. "What's stopping you from solving this problem yourself right now?"

### Objections & Concerns
16. "What worries you about adopting new training software?"
17. "Would your staff resist this? Why?"
18. "What ROI or payback period would you need to justify the investment?"

4. **Easiest to reach** (accessible decision-makers)
