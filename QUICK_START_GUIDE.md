## üöÄ Two Path Options

Choose based on your timeline and preference:

### Path A: In-Person First (Your Original Plan)
**Timeline:** 1-2 weeks to complete interviews
**Best for:** Local market, building strong relationships, immediate feedback

**Steps:**
1. Print 20-30 copies of [INTERVIEW_SHEET_1PAGE.md](INTERVIEW_SHEET_1PAGE.md)
2. Visit restaurants during off-hours (10am-2pm or 3-5pm)
3. Conduct on-the-spot or scheduled interviews
4. Transcribe recordings and share with me for analysis
5. Select beachhead segment after 20-30 interviews

### Path B: Email Outreach First (Scalable)
**Timeline:** 2-3 weeks to get 10-15 interviews scheduled
**Best for:** Broader geographic reach, more systematic approach

**Steps:**
1. Set up business email and SendGrid account
2. Build prospect list (50-100 restaurants)
3. Launch email campaign using templates
4. Schedule interviews with respondents
5. Conduct interviews via phone/video
6. Select beachhead after analysis

### Path C: Hybrid (Recommended)
**Timeline:** 2-3 weeks
**Best for:** Balancing speed with scale

**Steps:**
1. **Week 1:** Visit 10-15 local restaurants in person
2. **Week 1-2:** Set up email system, send to 50-100 non-local prospects
3. **Week 2-3:** Conduct phone interviews with email respondents
4. **Week 3:** Analyze all data (20-30 total interviews), select beachhead
5. **Week 4:** Invite 3-5 best-fit restaurants to pilot

## ‚ùì Decision Trees

### "Should I keep interviewing or start building?"

**Keep Interviewing if:**
- Less than 20 interviews completed
- No clear segment winner yet
- Fewer than 3 hot leads
- Feature priorities still unclear

**Start Building if:**
- 20+ interviews done
- Clear beachhead segment selected
- 3+ pilot commitments
- Top 3 MVP features validated
- WTP confirms viable business model

---

### "Should I pivot my segment/approach?"

**Consider Pivoting if:**
- After 30 interviews, less than 30% express strong interest
- WTP is consistently below $10/user (won't support business)
- Different segment shows 3x more enthusiasm
- Core assumption invalidated (e.g., they don't care about menu knowledge)

**Persevere if:**
- Strong interest from target segment (even if small number)
- Clear WTP at or above target price
- Solvable objections (e.g., "show me proof it works")
- Problem intensity is high (quantified pain)

1. **Start small, learn fast** - 10 great interviews beat 50 mediocre ones

8. **Share data with me often** - I can spot patterns you might miss
