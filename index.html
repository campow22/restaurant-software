Why I'm Building This

I'm Cameron, a student and aspiring entrepreneur with 7 years of fine dining experience at restaurants like Mission Hill Winery's Terrace Restaurant and Wildlight Kitchen & Bar. I've trained dozens of new staff and experienced the frustration of outdated training methods firsthand.

My goal: make AI work for restaurant workers, not against them. ServingSmart AI is about empowering people to be experts at their craft.

Training Takes Forever

New servers spend months learning the menu while managers juggle training with operations, leading to inconsistent results.

Costly Mistakes

Wrong orders, allergen errors, and service mistakes cost hundreds to thousands monthly in comps, remakes, and lost guests.

High Turnover

Poor training leads to overwhelmed staff who quit early, creating an expensive cycle of constant retraining.

No Clear Pathways

Talented support staff want to advance to serving roles but lack structured training to make the leap.

What We're Building

An AI-powered platform that makes training effortless—for managers and empowering for staff

AI-Generated Training

Upload your menu and service standards. Our AI creates comprehensive training materials automatically—no weeks of manual work.

Mobile-First Learning

Staff learn on their phones with engaging quizzes and flashcards. Study between shifts, master the menu faster.

Manager Visibility

Real-time dashboard shows exactly who knows what. Identify knowledge gaps before they become service errors.

Career Pathways

Clear progression from busser to server to lead. Structured certifications that recognize expertise and enable advancement.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the product ready to use?

Not yet. We're in the customer discovery phase, talking to restaurant managers to make sure we build exactly what you need. The platform will launch in April 2026 for pilot customers.

How much will it cost?

We're validating pricing through customer interviews, but we're estimating $15-25 per employee per month based on the value delivered. Pilot partners will receive significant discounts.

Why should I talk to you?

Your input directly shapes what we build. Plus, you'll get first access to the platform, exclusive pricing, and the opportunity to become a founding customer. No sales pitch—just a genuine conversation about training challenges.

How long is the interview?

15 minutes. It's a casual conversation about your current training process, biggest pain points, and what would make your life easier.

Will AI replace restaurant jobs?

No. ServingSmart AI is designed to empower restaurant workers, not replace them. We're using AI to handle the tedious work of creating training materials so people can focus on delivering exceptional hospitality. Our mission is to make restaurant jobs better and more rewarding.

What restaurants are you working with?

We're currently talking to fine dining, casual dining, and fast casual restaurants to determine which segment needs this most. Your restaurant could be one of our first pilot partners.
